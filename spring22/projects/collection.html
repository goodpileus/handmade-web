Class Collection

The class collection is located here in this are.na channel.

This will be a space to share interesting websites and resources related to the class. This is an optional but recommended exercise. Feel free to contribute things that you consider beneficial to the rest of the class—be it websites, readings, resources, images, or anything else that you come across online. Classes in the future will use this same collection, so by adding things to it you will be assisting your peers and those in the future. :-)

Are.na has a browser extension here. Not necessary, but helpful if you end up using are.na often.
