Class Collection

Over the course of the class I will ask you to occasionally contribute materials to the class collection on the are.na channel.

This will be a space to share resources related to the class. I will start by asking you to add a favorite website to the collection. Beyond this, feel free to contribute things that you consider beneficial to the rest of the class—be it websites, readings, resources, images, or anything else that you come across online.

Are.na has a bookmarklet and chrome extension here. Not necessary, but helpful if you end up using are.na often.
